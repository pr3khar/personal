moments

What is moments?

Moments, as the word means, is all about sharing the in-class moments of the children with their parents. It is designed to bridge the gap between what students do at Openhouse and the information communicated with parents. It will make parents feel more connected and excited as they see their children do and learn exciting things at Openhouse. We primarily aim to start with in-class pictures and videos , but down the line, aim to add other forms like text, stickers, milestones, etc.

There are two parts two it: first is the teacher's app, where the teacher records the moment and then share it and the second is the parent's side where the see moments for each of their child.
